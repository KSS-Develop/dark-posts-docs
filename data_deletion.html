Data Deletion Instructions

Instagram Dark Posts Creator Application

Important: This application does not store any personal data on our servers. All operations are performed in real-time using Meta's APIs, and no user information is retained by our system.

What Data Does This App Access?

The Instagram Dark Posts Creator only accesses business data that you explicitly authorize:

  • Facebook Page basic information (name, ID)
  • Instagram Business Account basic information (name, ID, username)
  • Ad Account information for creating advertising content
  • Permission to create unpublished posts on your behalf

How to Remove App Access (Data Deletion)

Since no data is stored on our servers, "data deletion" means revoking the app's access to your Facebook/Instagram accounts. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Access Facebook Settings

  1. Log into your Facebook account
  2. Click the dropdown arrow in the top-right corner
  3. Select "Settings & Privacy" → "Settings"

Step 2: Navigate to Apps and Websites

  1. In the left sidebar, click "Apps and Websites"
  2. You'll see a list of apps connected to your account

Step 3: Remove the App

  1. Find "Instagram Dark Posts Creator" or "DarkPost" in the list
  2. Click on the app name
  3. Click "Remove App"
  4. Confirm the removal

Alternative Method: Through Business Manager

If you're managing the app through Facebook Business Manager:

  1. Go to business.facebook.com
  2. Navigate to "Business Settings"
  3. Click "Apps" in the left sidebar
  4. Find "Instagram Dark Posts Creator" and click "Remove"

What Happens After Removal?

  • The app will no longer have access to your Facebook or Instagram accounts
  • No new content can be created by the app
  • Previously created dark posts will remain in your Facebook/Instagram accounts (you can delete them manually if desired)
  • All app permissions are immediately revoked

Additional Content Cleanup

To remove dark posts created by this app:

  1. Go to Facebook Business Manager → Content Library
  2. Filter by "Unpublished Posts"
  3. Manually delete any posts created by the app (they will have "Dark Post:" in the title)

Verification of Data Deletion

Confirmation: Once you remove the app from your Facebook settings, all access is immediately terminated. Since we don't store any data on our servers, this completes the data deletion process.
