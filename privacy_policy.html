Privacy Policy - Instagram Dark Posts Creator

Last updated: September 16, 2025

1. Overview

This privacy policy describes how the "Instagram Dark Posts Creator" application ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our service to create unpublished posts for advertising purposes on Facebook and Instagram.

2. Information We Collect

The App only accesses business data that you explicitly authorize, including:

  • Facebook Page ID and basic page information
  • Instagram Business Account ID and basic account information
  • Facebook Ad Account ID and permissions
  • Business Manager assets you explicitly grant access to

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Creating unpublished posts (dark posts) on your Facebook Page
  • Creating advertising content for your Instagram Business account
  • Accessing your Ad Account to create advertising creatives
  • Generating UTM tracking parameters for marketing analytics

4. Data Storage and Security

The App operates with a privacy-first approach:

  • No personal data is stored: All operations are performed in real-time
  • No user data retention: API calls are made directly to Meta's servers
  • Local processing only: Results are saved locally on your device
  • Secure connections: All API communications use HTTPS encryption

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to any third parties. The App only communicates with:

  • Facebook/Meta APIs to perform authorized actions
  • Your own business systems (Payload CMS) when explicitly configured

6. Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can revoke app permissions at any time via Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
  • You can delete any content created by the App through Facebook Business Manager
  • You control which business assets the App can access

7. Data Deletion

Since the App does not store any user data, there is no data to delete from our systems. To completely remove the App's access to your accounts:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
  2. Find "Instagram Dark Posts Creator" in your app list
  3. Click "Remove" to revoke all permissions

8. Compliance

This App complies with:

  • Meta's Platform Terms and Developer Policies
  • Facebook Marketing API Terms of Service
  • Instagram Platform Policy
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
